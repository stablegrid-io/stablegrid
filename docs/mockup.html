330/110kV Autotransformer Substation
"Thermal sensors on the 330/110kV autotransformer are reading 94°C — 12° over nominal. Evening Peak is pushing load through a unit not rated for sustained overload. You've got 41 hours before it trips offline."
What's happening
Autotransformers share a common winding between primary and secondary circuits. Sustained overload causes copper losses to generate heat beyond the oil's thermal capacity — eventually triggering the protection relay and taking the unit offline.
