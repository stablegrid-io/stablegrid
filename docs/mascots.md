# stableGrid Mascot Trio — "Grid Workers"

Three animated characters representing the progression through stableGrid's learning tracks. They're **electrical grid workers** — hard hats, utility belts, glowing tools — styled as friendly, modern 3D-rendered characters (Pixar-meets-Figma aesthetic). Each embodies a career stage in data engineering.

---

## 1. Volt — Junior Grid Worker

**Color accent:** Cyan/Teal (`#99F7FF` — stableGrid primary)

**Personality:** Eager, curious, slightly wide-eyed. The new recruit who just got their hard hat.

**Visual description:**

- Young-looking character with a slightly oversized hard hat that glows cyan at the brim
- Wearing a clean, fresh utility vest with a single glowing badge that reads "J1"
- Carries a small digital tablet/clipboard, studying it intently
- Has a basic multimeter tool clipped to their belt
- Expression: enthusiastic smile, bright eyes, leaning forward slightly
- Background element: a single power line stretching behind them, just connected
- Subtle particle effects: small cyan sparks floating around their hard hat
- Pose: one hand adjusting their hard hat, other hand holding the tablet at chest height

**Represents:** Core Foundation track — learning the basics, connecting their first circuits, building foundational understanding.

---

## 2. Ohm — Mid-Level Grid Engineer

**Color accent:** Amber/Gold (`#FFC965` — mid track color)

**Personality:** Confident, focused, hands-on problem solver. The reliable operator who keeps systems running.

**Visual description:**

- Medium build, standing with authority but approachable posture
- Hard hat fits perfectly, with an amber glow strip and a heads-up display visor flipped up
- Weathered utility vest with multiple tool loops filled — wrench, probe, cable ties
- Wears insulated gloves, one hand gripping a large glowing connector cable
- Belt has a holographic diagnostic screen projecting data streams
- Expression: calm half-smile, one eyebrow slightly raised — the "I've seen this before" look
- Background element: a junction box with multiple cables properly routed
- Subtle particle effects: amber energy pulses traveling along the cable they hold
- Pose: standing sideways, plugging a cable into a junction, looking back at the viewer confidently

**Represents:** Advanced Systems track — optimizing performance, managing complex joins and shuffles, keeping pipelines healthy.

---

## 3. Watt — Senior Grid Architect

**Color accent:** Red/Coral (`#FF716C` — senior track color)

**Personality:** Strategic, composed, the one everyone calls when the grid goes critical. A quiet authority.

**Visual description:**

- Taller, more composed posture, slight forward lean suggesting leadership
- Sleek hard hat with integrated red holographic HUD showing grid topology
- Streamlined utility coat (not vest) with clean lines and subtle grid-pattern stitching
- No hand tools visible — instead, both hands are outstretched with holographic grid maps floating between their palms
- Small drone companion hovers at shoulder height, projecting system status
- Expression: focused but calm, slight knowing smile, eyes scanning the holographic display
- Background element: an entire substation grid visible in miniature hologram
- Subtle particle effects: red energy arcs between fingertips and the holographic display
- Pose: standing central, arms slightly apart with the grid hologram between their hands, commanding the system

**Represents:** Platform Architecture track — enterprise governance, optimizer internals, designing systems that scale across teams and regions.
